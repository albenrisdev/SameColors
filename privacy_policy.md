# Privacy Policy — SameColors

**Effective Date:** March 15, 2026

## Overview

SameColors is a simple, offline color-matching puzzle game. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

---

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

SameColors has no internet connectivity, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no account system. No data is ever transmitted off your device.

---

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app saves a small amount of game data to your device using Android's `SharedPreferences` storage. This data never leaves your device and includes:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Selected difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard) | Remember your last chosen difficulty |
| High score per difficulty level | Display your personal best scores |
| Sound on/off preference | Remember your audio preference |
| Current game state (board layout, score) | Resume your game when you reopen the app |

This data is stored locally under the app's private storage and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- No names, email addresses, or contact information
- No location data
- No device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, etc.)
- No usage analytics or crash reports
- No advertising identifiers
- No camera, microphone, or storage access

---

## Permissions

SameColors requests **no special Android permissions**. The app does not access the internet, your contacts, your files, or any sensors.

---

## Children's Privacy

SameColors does not collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13. It is safe for all ages.

---

## Third-Party Services

SameColors does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media services.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version of the app, the updated policy will be made available on the app's store listing page. The effective date at the top of this document will be updated accordingly.

---

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project repository or contact the developer directly through the Google Play Store listing.
