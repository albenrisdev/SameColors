How to Play

Simple rules, endless strategy.

↔️

Swipe

Swipe in any direction to slide all tiles across the 4x4 grid.

🎨

Match

When same-colored tiles collide, they merge into one and your move counter resets.

⚡

Score

The more moves you have left when you merge, the higher your score reward.

🏆

Survive

Run out of moves or fill the board with no merges possible and it's game over.
